Signal Drift: Discover the World’s Most Obscure Radio Stations

1. Introduction
Signal Drift is a web app that curates the most obscure, under-listened, and offbeat radio stations from around the world. It aims to reverse the popularity paradigm — highlighting the least-played stations and elevating forgotten audio corners of the internet. Unlike apps that showcase trending music or high-traffic broadcasts, Signal Drift celebrates lowlights — the eerie, the eccentric, the quiet signals fading in the noise.

2. Product overview
Signal Drift connects to a free global radio directory (e.g., RadioBrowser API) and retrieves metadata about thousands of stations. It filters and ranks these by the lowest listener counts or fallback criteria (e.g., bitrate, region, obscurity tags). Users can browse or search for rare stations, tune in live, and bookmark favorites. Optional GPT-generated blurbs give poetic or speculative summaries of each station’s vibe.
